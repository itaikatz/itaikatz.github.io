Lacuna Labs is dedicated to novel solutions for audiovisual evidence analysis
Preparation
In an ideal world, audio and video data is noise-free and crystal clear. In the real world this is rarely the case: images are blurry, video is jumpy, and conversations are muffled. Our advanced mathematical techniques help recover the evidence you need from the data you have. Learn More
Analysis
When the original audio or video doesn't speak for itself, forensic techniques can provide needed measurements. Our analytical services draw on a diverse set of photogrammetric technqiues: 3D scene reconstruction, estimating vehicle speeds, face recognition, and others. Our analyses are explained in clear, concise language to ensure they can be understood confidently. Learn More
Presentation
Presenting proper degree of certainty
A primary goal of data visualization is to communicate information clearly and efficiently to users via the information graphics selected, such as tables and charts. Effective visualization helps users in analyzing and reasoning about data and evidence. It makes complex data more accessible, understandable and usable.
